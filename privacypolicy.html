Privacy Policy.

NLOC Labs is committed to the limited and responsible protection of user data in connection with our products, specifically the NLOC Labs Team Pins and the affiliated scoring system. We collect only the information necessary to facilitate product orders and manage the competitive team scoring environment. This data includes Registration Information (such as name and email required for purchases), Team Affiliation Data (your chosen house for score tabulation), and Usage Data (transaction history used to allocate points). This information is used strictly to process your orders, accurately calculate and publicly display the overall team scores, manage communications regarding your transactions, and maintain the security and operational integrity of our services. We do not engage in the sale of your personal data.

We prioritize the security of the information we collect and employ reasonable safeguards to protect it. Data is only shared with essential third parties, such as payment processors and shipping partners, or when legally mandated. Note that certain aggregated or non-personally identifiable scoring information may be publicly displayed to support the competitive ecosystem. If you have questions regarding the limited data we collect or our privacy practices, please contact our us at novakzhao24@nxschool.kiwi, linuszhang24@nxschool.kiwi, or oscarren24@nxschool.kiwi for clarification. We reserve the right to update this summary periodically, and any revisions will be posted here.
