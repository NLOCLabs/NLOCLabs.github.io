Product Safety and Compliance Notice

Please read and adhere to all guidelines regarding the use and storage of NLOC Labs products. Failure to follow these warnings may result in injury or property damage.

  • CHOKING HAZARD: This product contains small parts (the pin and magnetic backing) and is not a toy. It is **not suitable for children under 3 years** of age. Keep out of reach of infants and small children.
  • MAGNET WARNING: This product contains small magnets. Swallowed magnets can stick together across intestines causing serious infections and death. Seek immediate medical attention if magnets are swallowed or inhaled.
  • PACEMAKER WARNING: The strong magnets in this product may interfere with the function of pacemakers, ICDs, and other medical devices. Keep pins at least six (6) inches away from these devices. Consult a physician before use if you have any medical implant.
  • GENERAL USE: Do not use this product if damaged. Store the pin and backing securely when not in use.
