About Us.

Our Mission and Founding Principles.

NLOC Labs was founded on the singular vision of merging community spirit with tangible technology, transforming abstract concepts of loyalty and competition into real, measurable engagement. Born from the shared ambition of Novak Z, Oscar R, Linus Z, and Chenxi Z, the company was established to create high-quality, high-impact products that directly fuel organized community endeavors, starting with the House system pins. Our commitment is rooted in innovative design, engineering precision, and absolute transparency in the mechanism by which every purchase translates directly into critical points for your chosen team, ensuring fairness and driving the competitive spirit forward.
